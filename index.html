Credits

This site is the creation of siblings Dave Korns and Carol Korns Cassone. Dave is the web dev guru with ideas but weak grammer skills. Carol is the moral conscious, brains, and grammer expert of the duo. Ideas for posts come from anywhere and from either of us. We expect this will be a mixture of life stories, family values, lessons learned, lessons y'all need to learn, and a mix of snarky political rants. We hope you enjoy it. 

Credit for the ideas behind this website are numerous. First and foremost is Virgina H. Korns, our mother. When we didn't want to go to school in the morning we would pretend to be sick. Virginia would put a thermometer in our mouth then cross her arms and tamp her foot. After a few seconds she would pull it out, glance at it, and declare "It's all in your head. Now put on your guloshes and get out the door before you're late".

Last but not least, artistist style credit goes to iStockPhoto.com's Trilok. It was his iStock submission which inspired the current look and feel of the website. Who the heck creates a website with a red background and black and white text. Thank you Trilok, your work is inspirational and masterful.
